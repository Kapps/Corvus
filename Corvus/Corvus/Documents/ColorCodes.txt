Color code for damage text and status effects:
	Numbers (From damage or status effects) 
		White: Regular Damage
		Orange: Critical Hit
		Crimson: Entity is weak to that element.
		Navy: Entity is resistant to that element.
		Aqua: Healing 
		DarkBlue: ManaHeal
		Crimson: Might,
		Navy: Pierce, Slow, Weakness
		DarkViolet: Poison
		LightCyan: Recover (text part only)

	StatusEffect Icon
		LightSalmon: A positive effect.
		LightBlue: A negative effect.
